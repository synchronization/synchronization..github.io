Dear friends,

Walking challenge 12 will start on Wednesday January 24th and will last till Feb 23 (31 days). This is how it works:

  1. You put down $50
  2. We discard your three worst days in the challenge
  3. Your daily score is calculated as log(#daily_steps / 10000)
  4. We sum your daily scores to get your total score
  5. You win if your total score is not negative
  6. All winners will get $40 back
  7. The rest of the money ($10 from everyone and $40 from those who lost) will be distributed among the winners proportional to their total number of steps
  8. Don’t have Fitbit? No problem! Please update us daily of your device steps
  9. Technical difficulties? Please let one of us know ASAP
  10. How well are you doing? Click on your name (and others) and compare: https://synchronization.github.io/challenge/

Appendix: How do we calculate “average log steps”?

average log steps = EXP(SUM(LN(#DAILY_STEPS / 10000)) / (NUMBER_OF_DAYS))*10000

In which the SUM is calculated over the n-1 best days and NUMBER_OF_DAYS=n-1

We encourage you to make a copy of the spreadsheet and play with the formula to have a better sense of how it works.
